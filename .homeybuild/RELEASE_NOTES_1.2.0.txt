GeoZones 1.2.0

- Replaced the ambiguous default zone state "none" with "not_defined".
- Added explicit subject zone states: unknown and error.
- Removed inverse zones from the engine, settings UI and Flow definitions.
- Precomputed and stored zone area in areaM2 during configuration sanitization.
- Reserved not_defined, unknown and error for zone, group and category names.
- Simplified zone distance calculations to standard circles and polygons only.
