GeoZones lets you create circles and polygons on a map and use them in Homey flows. It helps you detect whether someone or something is at home, at work, at school or in any other place that matters to your automations.

Use GeoZones when you want reusable zones without creating virtual devices. The app can remember the last known zone for each subject, so your flows can react when someone enters, leaves or moves between the places you define.
